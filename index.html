Sujing Wang, Ph.D

Biographical Sketch

Sujing Wang received her Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from the University of Houston in 2014. She is an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at Lamar University, Beaumont TX. Her research interests include data mining, machine learning, big data, and with applications to health sciences and environmental sciences. Sujing is the faculty advisor of the StairStep Computer Science program at Lamar University.
